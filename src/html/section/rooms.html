GYM FACILITIES

We have boxing gear for rent for drop-in guests and top-of-the-line Panther Gear for those that are ready to get serious about their training.

Weight training

ENDURANCE, POWER, & EXPLOSIVENESS

Start your session off with some weight training to build physical strength and conditioning. Our equipment includes a variety of weightlifting machines and free weights that allow boxers to work on their endurance, power, and explosiveness. In this space, boxers can perform exercises that will help them improve their punching power, footwork, and overall athleticism.

The weight room is often a hub of activity in the gym, with boxers of all skill levels and experience levels working together to push themselves to their limits and become the best version of themselves.

Cardio room

AGILITY, COODINATION, & SPEED

Our cardio room contains a mixture of treadmills, stationary bikes, and ellipticals. This room is designed to help boxers improve their cardiovascular endurance, which is critical for sustaining high-intensity boxing workouts and fights.

Our jump ropes and medicine balls help boxers work on their agility, coordination, and speed. Cardio training is a key component of a boxer’s training regimen, as it helps them to maintain their energy levels throughout a fight and recover more quickly between rounds.

In the cardio room, boxers can work on their fitness goals in a focused and supportive environment, with access to the equipment they need to take their training to the next level.

CHANGE ROOMS & SHOWERS

CHANGE ROOMS & SHOWERS

Change rooms and lockers are an essential part of any boxing gym, providing a secure and private space for boxers to change and store their personal belongings during their workouts. The change room typically contains a row of lockers or cubbies, each assigned to a particular boxer, where they can store their gym bag, clothing, and any other personal items they bring to the gym.

We have separate change rooms for men and women, with separate shower and bathroom facilities. Having a dedicated space to change and store belongings can help boxers feel more comfortable and organized, allowing them to focus on their workouts without worrying about the safety of their personal belongings. It’s also a great opportunity for boxers to socialize and bond with their fellow gym members before and after their training sessions.

STEAM ROOM

RELAX & DETOXIFY

Steam rooms provide a range of health benefits, including relaxation, detoxification, and improved cardiovascular health. Boxers can use the steam room to help relieve sore muscles and joint pain after a tough workout, or to unwind and de-stress after a long day. The heat can also help to increase blood flow, which can improve cardiovascular health and boost the immune system.

Many boxers find that using the steam room regularly helps them to feel more relaxed and energized, and can even help them to sleep better at night. We consider it to be a vital gym facility, providing boxers with a space to unwind and rejuvenate their bodies and minds.

TRAINING CLASSES

TRAINING CLASSES

Panther Gym offers boxing classes, yoga classes, personal training, and nutrition counseling to provide a well-rounded and comprehensive approach to fitness and health. Boxing classes can help boxers to improve their technique, footwork, and conditioning, while also providing a challenging and enjoyable workout. Yoga classes can help boxers to improve their flexibility, balance, and mental focus, while also promoting relaxation and stress reduction.

Personal training can provide customized workout plans and one-on-one support, helping boxers to achieve their fitness goals more effectively. Nutrition counseling provides guidance on healthy eating habits, meal planning, and supplements to help boxers optimize their diets and fuel their bodies for peak performance.

By offering this range of services, we provide a holistic approach to health and fitness, while helping boxers to stay motivated and inspired, and to achieve their goals in a fun and engaging way.

WELLNESS SERVICES

MASSAGE, CHIRO, & ACUPUNCTURE

Panther Gym offers massage, chiropractor, and acupuncture services, all in-house. These services can provide a range of health benefits to boxers, helping them to recover faster from injuries, prevent future injuries, and improve their overall physical and mental well-being. Massage therapy can help to relieve muscle tension and soreness, increase flexibility and range of motion, and improve circulation.

Chiropractic care can help to realign the spine and joints, reducing pain and inflammation and improving overall mobility. Acupuncture can help to relieve pain and promote relaxation, helping boxers to reduce stress and anxiety and improve their mental focus. Having access to these services in one convenient location allows patrons to save time and money while taking care of their bodies. You DO NOT need to be a gym member to book these services.

Juice bar

REFUEL & RECOVER

Our in-house juice bar provides a convenient and healthy way for boxers to refuel and recover after their workouts. Juices and smoothies made from fresh fruits, vegetables, and protein powder can help to provide a quick and easy source of energy, while also providing important nutrients and vitamins to support overall health and wellness.

Our members appreciate the convenience of having a juice bar in their gym, as it allows them to quickly grab a healthy snack or meal before or after their workouts. The juice bar also serves as a social hub within the gym, providing a space for boxers to relax and connect with their fellow gym members.

DIFFERENTLY ABLED

WHEELCHAIR ELEVATOR ACCESS

Panther Gym is a welcoming and accessible environment for people of all abilities. Our wheelchair elevator provides access to all areas of the gym, including the weight room & cardio room located upstairs, ensuring that everyone can participate in the activities and workouts offered.

By offering an inclusive environment, the gym promotes diversity and supports the health and wellness of all members of the community by making it easier for differently abled individuals to engage in physical activity and achieve their fitness goals. Our facility was consciously built to keep accessibility and inclusivity in mind.
